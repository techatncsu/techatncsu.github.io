Mission

Our mission is to equip Computer Science & Electrical Engineering students at NC State with the knowledge and connections they need to get the best technical job. We aim to create a collaboration where students can share and learn from the technical and interview experiences of their peers, so as to assist others in achieving similar career goals. Towards this we provide workshops, tech talks, and recruitment connections.
